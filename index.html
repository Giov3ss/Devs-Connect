About us

This is a meetup club for digital professionals, students and enthusiasts to share their insights into a new software technologies with each other. We'll arrange talks from communnity members who wants to share their own experiences when using new technologies or frameworks, professionally, academically or personally. We aim to create a space for people from varying levels of expertise in software development to come together and share knowledge based on a culture of inclusion and collaboration. It's also an opportunity to meet new friends, talk about your own project, find new opportunities and of course have fun.
